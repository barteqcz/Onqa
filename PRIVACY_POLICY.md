# Privacy Policy for Onqa

Onqa ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Location Data
Onqa is designed to help you discover radio stations near your current location. To provide this service, the app requires access to your device's location.
- **Precise Location:** We use your GPS/fine location to accurately determine which radio stations are available in your area.
- **Background Location:** We access your location while the app is in the background to keep your local radio discovery updated as you move.

### Usage Data
We do not collect personal identifiers, contact information, or browsing history. The app may store technical preferences locally on your device (such as your favorite stations and theme settings) using Android Jetpack DataStore.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected location data solely for the purpose of:
- Identifying and displaying radio stations relevant to your current geographical area.
- Providing a localized "ether" discovery experience.

**We do not sell, trade, or rent your location data to third parties.**

## 3. Third-Party Services
While the app itself does not collect personal data for its own use, it utilizes third-party services that may have their own privacy policies:
- **Google Play Services (Location):** Used to retrieve location data.
- **Radio Stream Providers:** When you listen to a radio station, your device connects directly to the broadcaster's stream. These third-party broadcasters may receive your IP address as part of the standard technical process of streaming audio.

## 4. Data Retention
Location data is used in real-time to fetch radio station lists and is not stored on our servers. Any preferences or "Favorite" stations you save are stored locally on your device and remain there until you choose to delete them or uninstall the app.

## 5. Your Choices
- **Location Permissions:** You can enable or disable location access at any time through your device's system settings. However, disabling location will make the app unavailable.

## 6. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored locally on your device. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this repository.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
